Latitude vs. Temperature

There is a clear correlation between nearness-to-equator and temperature - the closer you get to the equator, the higher the max temperature in the city.

This confirms what is generally expected - countries like Singapore, Brazil, and Kenya, which are near or on the equator, generally have higher daily temperatures than countries like Canada or Norway.
